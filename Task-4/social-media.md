## ✅ Part B — Social Media Do's and Don'ts

| | Rule |
|---|------|
| ✅ |  Always update and keep your Github and LinkedIn accounts with accurate and relevant information |
| ✅ | Think before you post. Ask yourself: would I be comfortable if my professor or future employer saw this? |
| ✅ | Use strong and unique passwords for all social media accounts – to protect the data |
| ✅ | Maintain a positive and respectful tone while interacting with other people |
| ✅ | Limit the amount of personal information like your phone number , address – to protect your identity and security online |
| ❌ | Don't post complaints about college, faculty, or classmates publicly |
| ❌ | Don't share your college ID or registration number on any public platform |
| ❌ | Don't accept connection requests from people you do not know personally, especially on professional platforms like LinkedIn  |
| ❌ | Don't Spread unverified news or forward messages without checking the source |
| ❌ | Don't use social media to settle personal disputes — always resolve conflicts privately and respectfully |
