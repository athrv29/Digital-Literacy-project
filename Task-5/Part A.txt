Part A — Case Study: Cyberbullying
Crime TypeCyberbullyingTarget ProfileFirst-year college students in IndiaPrimary PlatformWhatsApp, Instagram, Online Gaming ChatsTypical ImpactEmotional distress, anxiety, reputation damageReport Channelcybercrime.gov.in  |  Helpline: 1930
How It Happened — Step by Step
Riya, a first-year college student, began receiving messages from an unknown Instagram account. She then discovered that some of her photos had been taken from her profile, edited, and shared in group chats with insulting captions. A friend later showed her these posts, which made her realize the situation had spread beyond private messages. Shocked and embarrassed, Riya understood that she had become a target of cyberbullying.
Step 1 — Targeting: The bully identified her through her public profile and posts.
Step 2 — Fake Identity: A fake account was created to stay anonymous.
Step 3 — Initial Contact: The bully started sending sarcastic and offensive messages.
Step 4 — Escalation: Hurtful comments were posted on her photos, and private screenshots were misused.
Step 5 — Public Humiliation: Edited images and memes mocking her were shared in groups and stories.
Step 6 — Impact & Action: Riya felt stressed and anxious but eventually reported the account using platform tools and cybercrime portals. She filed a report on cybercrime.gov.in but no recovery was possible.

Reflection (100–150 words)
What surprised me most while researching this topic was how precisely these scams are engineered. In Riya’s case, the harassment wasn’t just random insults; it was a calculated attempt to isolate her by exploiting her social circles and personal insecurities. The way digital platforms can be weaponized to spread misinformation so rapidly is genuinely unsettling—it highlights that cyberbullying is often a deliberate, persistent campaign designed to exert power over a specific individual rather than a one-off disagreement.
The one habit I will personally change as a result of this task : I will strictly verify the context of any sensitive information shared about others before engaging with it and will immediately report abusive content through official platform channels. The few minutes it takes to report a post could prevent a situation from escalating into a crisis.
