| **Platform** | WhatsApp, Instagram, Online Gaming Chats |

| **Report Channel** |cybercrime.gov.in  |  Helpline: 1930 |

---

## 📖 Case Study Summary

Riya was targeted by a cyberbully who used a fake account to harass her through offensive messages and public humiliation using edited photos. Despite the significant emotional distress and the lack of content recovery, she took decisive action by reporting the perpetrator to both Instagram and the national cybercrime portal.

**How it happened — step by step:**
 1. **Targeting** - The bully identified her through her public profile and posts.
 2. **Fake Identity**- A fake account was created to stay anonymous.
 3. **Initial Contact** - The bully started sending sarcastic and offensive messages.
 4. **Escalation** - Hurtful comments were posted on her photos, and private screenshots were misused.
 5. **Public Humiliation** - Edited images and memes mocking her were shared in groups and stories.
 6. **Impact & Action** - Riya felt stressed and anxious but eventually reported the account using platform tools and cybercrime portals. She filed a report on cybercrime.gov.in but no recovery was possible.
